I am a postdoctoral researcher based at Stanford University’s Hopkins Marine Station. In general, I study the drivers of spatial and temporal change in marine ecosystems.

Currently, I am using the Adriatic Sea as a case study to examine the consequences of fisheries closures in the context of a diverse group of stakeholders, including conservation advocates, the fishermen themselves, and other economic sectors.
