I am a postdoctoral researcher based at Stanford University’s Hopkins Marine Station and the University of British Columbia.

I study marine ecosystems -  in particular, I am interested in how and why communities and populations vary in space and time.  I have tackled this broad question on tropical and temperate reefs, using a variety of field approaches, statistical and demographic models, and data syntheses.

Currently, I am working on the following projects, in collaboration with many colleagues:

  1. A global synthesis of biodiversity trends in coastal communities

  2. >The demography of body size decline in a temperate coral in the context of warming

  3. A review of resilience in marine foundation species in response to climatic disturbance
